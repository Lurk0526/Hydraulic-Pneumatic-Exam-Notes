## 10. Cavitation

(1) Def: If the pressure at a point is reduced far enough, hydraulic oil will vaporize and vapour cavities will be formed in the liquid, which air will separated from oil and result in large number of air bubble.

(2) Principle: The phenomenon of cavitation happens easily in suction of pump and valve orifice.

(3) How to decrease:
в‘  Reducing pressure drop, PвӮҒ/PвӮӮ < 1.5.
в‘Ў Design it is better for trying to avoid narrow, elbow or suddenly direction changed.
в‘ў Try to reduce the suction height of pump.
в‘Ј Good seal part to avoid air entrance.
в‘Ө Resist corrosive material used to try to keep the element rigidity and increasing exterior roughness for withstanding corrosion.

## 13. Oil trapping (Gear pump)

(1) Def: If the gear mesh overlap coefficient is greater than 1, some oil will be trapped between the contact point A,B. The enclosed volume changes with the gear rotation, resulting in oil trapping.

(2) Oil trapping may lead to hydraulic shock or cavitation due to the changes in the enclosed volume size.

(3) How to solving: relief groove.
